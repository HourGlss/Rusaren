# Class: Mage (WoW Mage / FF Black Mage vibes)

Identity:
- High-impact spells with positioning and cast discipline.
- Strongest payoff for being still.

## Tier 1: "Arcane Focus"
Grants:
- Weapon: staff (cosmetic) + baseline melee: Staff Bonk (Instant, weak, short range)
- Baseline spell: Arcane Bolt (CastTime, short, single-hit bolt)
- Movement profile: normal (no bonus)

## Tier 2 (core identity): "Frost Imprint"
Mechanic:
- Spell hits apply Chill (slow)
- Chill stacks to 3 and applies Root at max stacks

## Tier 3 (power passive): "Clearcasting"
Passive (big):
- After remaining still long enough to arm the passive, your next CastTime spell:
  - cannot be interrupted once
  - costs no mana

This makes “discipline to stand still” the mage’s defining power.

## Tier 4: "Blink"
Ability:
- Instant short teleport (escape + reposition)
- Does not break the server rule that CastTime/Channel require stillness (Blink is instant)

## Tier 5 (ultimate): "Meteor"
Ability (CastTime with heavy telegraph):
- Long-ish cast while still; huge AoE impact on completion
- Counterplay: interrupt, move out of telegraph, punish the stationary mage
