# Class: Rogue (WoW Rogue / FF Ninja vibes)

Identity:
- Burst melee assassin with control and dirty tricks.
- Plays around StealthZones and interrupts.

## Tier 1: "Twin Blades Stance"
Grants:
- Weapon: dual blades
- Baseline melee: Quick Slash (Instant, short range, low cooldown)
- Movement profile: +dash charge or slightly higher base speed

Notes:
- Quick Slash exists even if the player later pivots into spell trees.

## Tier 2 (core identity): "Poisoned Edge"
Mechanic:
- Weapon hits apply Poison (short duration DoT)
- Poison stacks up to 5 per source player

Counterplay:
- Cleanse/HoT sustain, armor, or disengage
- Reveal effects punish bush abuse and stealth play

## Tier 3 (power passive): "Opportunist"
Passive:
- Bonus damage vs targets affected by ANY negative status (Poison/Chill/Root/etc.)
OR
- First hit on a newly-seen target (emerging from fog/bush) deals bonus damage

This is the “build anchor” that rewards timing and positioning.

## Tier 4: "Shadowstep Kick"
Ability:
- Shadowstep (Instant): short teleport to enemy within range
- Kick (Instant): interrupt and apply short Silence (anti-caster tool)

Design note:
- Keep it fair: range limited, clear VFX, cooldown meaningful.

## Tier 5 (ultimate): "Execution Window"
Ability: "Assassinate"
- Short cast while still; if completes, massive single-target damage
- Fails if interrupted or you move
This should swing a round if set up, but is punishable by interrupts/spacing.
