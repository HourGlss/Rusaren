# Class: Bard (FF / EQ support skirmisher)

Identity:
- Tempo-based support-controller who speeds allies up and disrupts clustered enemies.
- Less raw healing than Cleric, more rhythm, spacing, and teamfight control.

Passive identity:
- `Encore`: songs linger and reward players who keep the tempo of the fight.
- This is authored class identity for future passive support, not a standalone runtime rule yet.

Current authored combat profile:
- ranged sonic poke
- mobility dash
- nearby haste anthem
- silence beam
- burst stun finisher
