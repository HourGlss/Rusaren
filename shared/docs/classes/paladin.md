# Class: Paladin (WoW Paladin / FF holy knight)

Identity:
- Frontline holy bruiser with controlled engages and emergency support.
- Wants to stand in the fight, punish exposed lanes, and stabilize nearby allies.

Passive identity:
- `Oathbound`: after committing to a fight, the paladin becomes harder to peel off the objective.
- This is design intent for later passive support, not a separate runtime system yet.

Current authored combat profile:
- melee beam pressure
- dash engage with stun
- close support via heal + haste pulse
- anti-caster silence burst
- heavy finishing beam
