# Class: Warrior (WoW Warrior / FF Dragoon-ish bruiser)

Identity:
- Durable melee engager that wins through pressure and control.
- Less trickery, more “you are not leaving”.

## Tier 1: "Arms Stance"
Grants:
- Weapon: sword/axe (two-handed)
- Baseline melee: Heavy Strike (Instant, medium cooldown, higher damage)
- Movement profile: short charge or slightly higher acceleration

## Tier 2 (core identity): "Battle Momentum"
Mechanic:
- Hitting with melee grants Momentum stacks
- Momentum increases move speed and/or damage slightly (short duration, refreshes)

Feels like a WoW rage/builder without adding a full resource system yet.

## Tier 3 (power passive): "Unbreakable"
Passive:
- Damage reduction while moving toward enemies

## Tier 4: "Grapple"
Ability:
- Instant: short-range yank if within melee range

Counterplay:
- dodge/spacing, stay outside melee yank range

## Tier 5 (ultimate): "Earthshatter"
Ability (Channel):
- Channel to slam the ground; each tick applies damage + brief slow in an area
- Stops on movement/interrupt

Round-swinging if you control space, punishable if enemies interrupt or kite.
