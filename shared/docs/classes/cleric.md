# Class: Cleric (WoW Priest/Paladin + FF White Mage vibes)

Identity:
- Sustain + buffs with “stand and cast” moments.
- Can brawl in melee but prefers enabling.

## Tier 1: "Mace and Prayer"
Grants:
- Weapon: mace + baseline melee: Smite Swing (Instant, modest)
- Baseline support spell: Minor Heal (CastTime)
- Movement profile: normal

## Tier 2 (core identity): "Sanctify"
Mechanic:
- Your heals also grant a brief buff (move speed, damage reduction, or cleanse-lite)
- Or: “Blessed Strikes” converts a small portion of melee damage into healing on self

## Tier 3 (power passive): "Renewing Aura"
Passive (strong):
- Constant small HoT on yourself
AND/OR
- Allies you heal gain HoT for a few seconds
Important: vision is NOT shared; this is purely combat.

## Tier 4: "Cleanse / Dispel"
Ability:
- Remove one negative status (Poison/Chill/Silence/etc.)
- CastTime version is more powerful; Instant version is weaker/longer cooldown

## Tier 5 (ultimate): "Divine Intervention"
Ability:
- Instant area effect
- Immediate large heal / protection effect on the affected zone or targets
Counterplay: spacing, baiting, and forcing poor timing before the cast is used.
